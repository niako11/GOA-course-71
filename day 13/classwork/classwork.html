About the Film - Inception

Plot: Inception follows Dom Cobb, a skilled thief who specializes in stealing secrets from within people's dreams. He is given a chance at redemption if he can successfully plant an idea in someone's mind – a process known as inception.

Director: Christopher Nolan

Cast:

  • Leonardo DiCaprio as Dom Cobb
  • Joseph Gordon-Levitt as Arthur
  • Elliot Page as Ariadne
  • Tom Hardy as Eames
  • Ken Watanabe as Saito

Release Year: 2010

Genre: Sci-Fi, Thriller
