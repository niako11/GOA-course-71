Welcome to Georgia

About Georgia

Georgia is a small country between Europe and Asia. It is in the South Caucasus region. The capital city is Tbilisi.

Nature

Georgia has beautiful mountains, green valleys, and the Black Sea coast. The Caucasus Mountains are perfect for hiking and skiing.

Culture

Georgians are very friendly. The country has old churches, castles, and traditional music and dance. The Georgian alphabet is unique and very beautiful.

Food

Georgian food is delicious. Try khachapuri (cheese bread) and khinkali (meat dumplings). Georgian wine is also very famous.

Why Visit Georgia?

  • Amazing nature
  • Rich history and culture
  • Tasty food and wine
  • Warm and kind people
